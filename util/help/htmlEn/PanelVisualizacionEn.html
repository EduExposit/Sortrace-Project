Visualization panel

The visualization panel It consists of two clearly differentiated parts:


Vector

The central part of the panel shows the state of the vector at each moment of execution. The variables that intervene in each moment of the process are highlighted with colors, configurable in the menu. Configuration, in the Visualization section.


Execution information

The part on the right shows additional information to understand better operation and efficiency of the algorithm, such as; he columns (number of algorithm iterations), or the number of comparisons, and position assignments that have been made so far.
